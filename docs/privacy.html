Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

The short version: DailyArc stores all your data on your device. We don't collect it, we don't see it, we don't sell it. Your data is yours.

What Data DailyArc Stores

DailyArc stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Habit names, schedules, and completion logs
  • Mood scores, energy levels, and activity tags
  • Journal entries and notes
  • App preferences (theme, accent color, notification settings)
  • Streak and badge progress

Where Your Data Lives

On your device. Only your device.

DailyArc does not use cloud storage, servers, or any remote infrastructure. All data is stored locally using Apple's on-device storage frameworks. There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no cloud sync.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect, transmit, store, or process:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Health data (HealthKit data is accessed on-device only and never transmitted)
  • Any data from other apps on your device

Third-Party Services

DailyArc does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

HealthKit

If you choose to enable HealthKit integration, DailyArc reads health metrics (such as step count or sleep duration) to auto-log habits. This data is read on-device only and is never transmitted off your device. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in your device's Settings.

Notifications

DailyArc may send local notifications (reminders, streak alerts) if you enable them. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Data Export and Deletion

You can export all your data as JSON at any time from Settings. You can delete all your data from within the app at any time. When you delete data or uninstall the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

DailyArc is intended for users aged 13 and older. During onboarding, we verify the user's birth year. Users under 13 are blocked from using the app in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

GDPR Rights (European Users)

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you inherently have full control over your data:

  • Right to access: All your data is visible within the app at all times
  • Right to portability: Export your data as JSON from Settings
  • Right to erasure: Delete all data from Settings at any time
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw data processing consent in Settings, which pauses all data processing

Data controller: DailyArc. Contact: privacy@dailyarc.app

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

DailyArc does not sell personal information. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no data to sell. A "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" option is available in Settings for transparency.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since DailyArc does not collect email addresses, we cannot notify you directly of changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or DailyArc's data practices, contact us at privacy@dailyarc.app.
