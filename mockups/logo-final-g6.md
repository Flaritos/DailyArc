# DailyArc Logo — Final Selection: G6 "Arc with Milestone Dot"

## Mark Description
A clean, uniform-width 270° arc in the brand gradient (#2563EB → #5F27CD) with a single filled circle at the END of the arc. The dot represents "you are here NOW" — the present moment. The arc behind is your history. The gap ahead is the future.

## Specifications
- **Arc:** 270° (gap at upper-right, ~45° position)
- **Stroke:** Uniform width, 14px at full size (1024px canvas)
- **Stroke caps:** Round
- **Gradient:** Linear, 135° angle, #2563EB (start) → #5F27CD (end)
- **Dot:** Filled circle at arc terminus, diameter ~1.8x stroke width (25px at full size)
- **Dot color:** Matches gradient at that endpoint (#5F27CD)
- **Gap direction:** Upper-right, suggesting forward/upward progress
- **Padding:** 15% from canvas edge

## App Icon
- White background (#FFFFFF) in iOS superellipse
- Alternative: warm-tinted (#F8F7F4) background
- Dark variant: #0F0F1A background with subtle glow on arc
